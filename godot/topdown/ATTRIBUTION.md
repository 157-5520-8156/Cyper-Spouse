# Top-down room assets

This prototype deliberately does not use generated room imagery.

- `assets/third_party/kenney/tiny-town/` is [Kenney Tiny Town](https://kenney.nl/assets/tiny-town), released under CC0 1.0. Its bundled `License.txt` is kept beside the files.
- `assets/third_party/opengameart/modern-houses/tiletest.png` is **Modern Houses Tileset TopDown** by Ritpop, downloaded from [OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/content/modern-houses-tileset-topdown), released under CC0 1.0. `LICENSE.txt` contains the CC0 legal text.
- `assets/characters/zhizhi-topdown.png` is an existing project-owned four-direction character sprite copied from `assets/pixel_agents/characters/zhizhi-base.png`.

The initial furniture silhouettes are code-native placeholders so that dimensions, collisions, and interaction anchors can be validated before choosing a final compatible interior tileset. They are not generated images and can be replaced object-by-object without changing navigation or scene data.
